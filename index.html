Privacy Policy

Podisodic Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

Terms of Service · Contact

Podisodic collects and processes limited information to operate the app, deliver features such as notifications and feedback handling, and improve quality, reliability, and user experience.

Information We Collect

  • Analytics on App activity (e.g. screens viewed, app opens)
  • Analytics on Podcast metadata (e.g. podcast title, podcast author)
  • Analytics on Technical metadata (e.g. app version, platform, device/app identifiers provided by analytics SDKs)
  • Anonymous app identifiers we generate to operate backend features (for example, an app-specific user ID and install ID)
  • Notification-related device metadata if notifications are enabled (for example, push token, platform, app version, locale, and timezone)
  • Subscription sync data used to support notification features (for example, feed URLs, podcast titles, and notification preference status)
  • Feedback and feature request message content that you choose to submit to us through the app

Information Stored On Your Device

  • Podcast subscriptions and settings
  • Playback history and resume positions
  • Cached episode metadata and artwork references
  • Playlists and downloaded episode file paths
  • Downloaded podcast audio files when you choose to download episodes for offline playback

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect in Analytics

  • Message content submitted in feedback text boxes
  • Email addresses, phone numbers, passwords, auth tokens, or secrets in analytics event properties

How We Use Information

  • To understand feature usage and improve product decisions
  • To diagnose bugs and improve reliability/performance
  • To monitor app stability and lifecycle behavior
  • To register devices for new-episode notifications and deliver those notifications when enabled
  • To sync subscription state needed for backend notification features
  • To receive, review, and respond to feedback and feature requests you send us

Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate parts of Podisodic. These providers may process information on our behalf over encrypted connections (HTTPS), including:

  • PostHog for product analytics
  • Supabase for backend storage and sync related to notifications and feedback
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notification delivery
  • Resend for forwarding feedback and feature request emails to support

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, Podisodic may register your device with a push token and related metadata such as platform, app version, locale, and timezone so we can deliver new episode notifications. You can disable notifications through your device settings or in-app notification controls where available.

Feedback And Support Messages

If you submit feedback or a feature request through the app, the message content you provide is stored so we can review it and may also be forwarded to our support inbox. We do not include that message text in analytics event properties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be processed by our service providers to operate notifications, analytics, backend sync, and support workflows, and is not used for advertising.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for product improvement, debugging, notification operations, feedback handling, and other operational purposes.

OPML Import And Export

If you import or export subscriptions using OPML, the file is created or processed on your device and then handled through the system file picker or share sheet you choose to use.

Your Choices

If you want to request deletion of data associated with your usage, contact us at: help.podisodic@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Podisodic is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
