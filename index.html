Privacy Policy

Podisodic Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Podisodic collects limited analytics data to improve app quality, reliability, and user experience.

Information We Collect

  • App activity analytics (for example, screens viewed and interaction events)
  • Podcast interaction metadata (for example, podcast title, podcast author, and feed URL when subscribing/unsubscribing)
  • Technical metadata for analytics (for example, app version, platform, and device/app identifiers provided by analytics SDKs)

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect in Analytics

  • Message content submitted in feedback text boxes
  • Email addresses, phone numbers, passwords, auth tokens, or secrets in analytics event properties

How We Use Information

  • To understand feature usage and improve product decisions
  • To diagnose bugs and improve reliability/performance
  • To monitor app stability and lifecycle behavior

Analytics Provider

We use PostHog as our analytics processor. Analytics events are sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Analytics data is processed by our service provider (PostHog) to operate and improve the app, and is not used for advertising.

Data Retention

We retain analytics data only as long as needed for product improvement, debugging, and operational purposes.

Your Choices

If you want to request deletion of data associated with your usage, contact us at: help.episode@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Podisodic is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.


If you have questions, contact: help.episode@gmail.com
